B I O

Ann Lei is a photographer from Taiwan. She take product and portrait photography for companies. Ann has a bachelor degree in Management Information Systems from National Chung Hsing University, which is one of the top ten colleges in Taiwan. She combines her technology background with her artistic insight into her career. She is a leader in the planning and creation of visual media. Ann is now studying her MFA in Visual Communication Design in Arizona State University, to become a designer who can do both photography and design herself in the future.

Since 2012, Ann has been to different countries, such as India, Nepal, China, as a volunteer. She enjoys spreading the love and kindness to the world, just seeing the smiles on other’s face means the world to her. Along meeting alternative people in those underdeveloped countries further developed how she determined to tell the stories of different parts of the world to others. That is the time she decided to practice in visual communication design, where she can fully understand how to communicate well through design and attract people’s attention efficiently.

Ann does everything with a purpose. She knows that she has to learn everything, not just the things she is interested in, to get to the place she wants to be. She started learning photography when she was in high school, self-taught everything about the colors, angles and technical skills. She advanced learning about film herself to editing when she was in college. Now she can create beautiful contents in websites or apps without other’s help. Every extra works she did is for the purpose to become a designer that can successfully understand client’s need, and create a homogeneous product for the brand.

In her spare time, Ann watches tutorial about design, photography and video editing skills on Youtube. She basically bury herself in the visual media world every single second, but somehow she really enjoy asphyxiating herself in this world and she would love to stay as long as she can.
